**Detector/data structure clarified:**
- Full-frame FLC: 2 SCI extensions (WFC1 + WFC2, each 2048×4096 px); AstroDrizzle
  mosaics both into a single ~9974×9974 DRC at 0.03"/pix
- Subarray WFC1B-2K FLC: 1 SCI extension only (2048×2048 px); DRC is ~4000–4800 px
- 8 subarray FLCs = 4 full-frame FLCs in data content (same number of dither pointings)
- The 4 advisor "datasets" per subarray filter = 4 CR-split associations × 2 exposures
  each = 8 individual FLC files; counts are correct
- Subarray POSTARG ≈ (41", 43") is the aperture mechanical offset, not a science dither;
  all 8 FLC files have identical CRVAL confirming they cover the same quasar field
- No "second half" exists for subarray targets — WFC2 was not read out; subarray DRC
  IS the complete science image
